Product Info

Description

The Aqara Motion Sensor P1 is a compact and highly sensitive motion detection device designed for smart home automation. It features a wide detection range of up to 7 meters (23 feet) and a 170-degree field of view, enabling it to detect movement with precision. The sensor utilizes infrared technology for fast and accurate motion sensing, and it is equipped with advanced algorithms to reduce false alarms. Compatible with Aqara’s ecosystem, it integrates seamlessly with various smart home hubs and systems, allowing users to automate lighting, security, and other devices based on detected motion. The sensor is also energy-efficient, with a long battery life, making it ideal for home security and smart home environments.

Key Features

  • Configurable Detection Timeout: Set detection timeouts from 1 to 200 seconds with no hardware modding; shorter timeouts reduce battery life.
  • 5-Year Battery Life: Achieve up to 5 years of battery life, even in low temperatures or with fast detection timeouts.
  • All-Round Compatibility: Supports HomeKit, Alexa, IFTTT, and more; Matter support via OTA update after specification release.
  • 170° Field of View: Wide 170° view for up to 2 meters and 150° for 7 meters, with adjustable angle via a 360° stand.
  • Adjustable Sensitivity: Choose from three sensitivity modes to reduce false alarms and enhance installation flexibility.
  • Built-in Light Sensor: Control lights based on motion and ambient light to save energy and extend bulb life.
  • Smart Home Automations: Integrate with smart home systems to automate tasks like lighting, curtains, or TV control.
  • Local and Remote Alarm: Activate local alarms and cloud notifications through the Aqara Home Alert System or HomeKit.
  • Zigbee 3.0 Protocol: Enjoy fast, stable, energy-efficient performance with improved compatibility and support.
  • Futureproof via OTA: Receive updates with new features and fixes via Over-The-Air (OTA) updates.

Note:
* Brightness cannot be detected seperately from motion and cannot be used in automations or exposed to Homekit.

Automated Lighting Control:

When motion is detected in the dark, the Motion Sensor P1 automatically triggers the smart light to turn on . If no motion is detected for a set period, the light will turn off.
