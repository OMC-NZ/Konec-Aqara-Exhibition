Product Info

Description

The Aqara Camera Hub G2H Pro is a smart home camera and hub in one. It offers video intercom, motion and sound detection, secure recording, and privacy controls. As a Zigbee hub, it connects with other Aqara devices for full home security and automation, and it supports HomeKit Secure Video for Apple users.

Key Features

  • Built-in Zigbee 3.0 Hub: Connect up to 128 Aqara devices for home automation, remote access, and third-party integration.
  • Enhanced HomeKit Support: Works with HomeKit Secure Video*, two-way audio, and all HomeKit Security System modes.
  • Wide Compatibility: Supports HomeKit, Alexa, Google Home, and streams to smart displays.
  • High-Capacity Storage: Supports microSD (up to 512 GB) and local NAS storage (Samba protocol).
  • Custom Ringtones & Timelapse: Set custom ringtones and create timelapse videos.
  • Alerts & Siren: Get alerts without subscription; includes a built-in siren for motion/sound detection.
  • IR Night Vision: 940 nm LEDs enable clear, non-distracting night vision.
  • 146° Wide-Angle & 1080p: Features a sharp 1080p sensor with a wide "fisheye" view.
  • 360° Mount: Magnetic base and mounting options for flexible placement.
  • Event Timeline: Marks events like door openings on the video timeline (with Aqara sensors).
  • Privacy & Activity Zones: Set activity zones and mask privacy areas.
  • Face & Package Recognition: Recognizes faces and packages with HomeKit Secure Video*.
  • RGB LED: Features a bright RGB indicator light

Note:
* An iCloud subscription, a HomeKit hub, and updated iOS/iPad OS/tvOS are required to support HomeKit Secure Video.
** Using NAS storage requires a supported microSD card and SMB1.0-compatible software.
*** Aqara sensors required for this functionality.
