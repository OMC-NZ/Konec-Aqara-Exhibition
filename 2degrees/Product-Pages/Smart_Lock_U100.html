Product Info

Description

The Aqara U100 is a smart deadbolt lock designed to seamlessly fit into your lifestyle. With multiple unlocking options like Apple Home, Home Key, fingerprints, and keypads, it adapts to your preferences for maximum convenience. Security is top-notch, meeting BHMA Level 3 standards, while features like automatic locking and customizable modes enhance usability. Its sleek, elegant design complements any home decor, and with up to 8 months of battery life and an IP65 waterproof rating, it's built to last. Plus, it can trigger other smart devices, making it the perfect addition to your smart home setup

Key Features

  • Apple Home & Home Key Support: Open doors effortlessly with the Apple Home App or Apple Watch. No keys needed—just tap to unlock your smart home.
  • Fingerprint Reader & Keypad: Unlock in 0.5 seconds using your fingerprint or password. No phone or key required. Secure with a frosted surface to prevent peeking and the option for temporary passwords for visitors.
  • Auto Lock & Door Sensing: Built-in gyroscope tracks door status, sending reminders to close it and automatically locking when shut.
  • Aqara & Third-Party Integrations: Compatible with Google Home, Alexa, IFTTT, and more via Aqara Zigbee 3.0 hub, enabling seamless automation like unlocking via Aqara video doorbell.
  • Security & Durability: Robust catalpa alloy shell, anti-peep backlit keyboard, and secure unlocking methods ensure top-tier protection.
  • Emergency Unlock Options: Use USB-C for temporary power or a mechanical key if the battery is dead, ensuring you're never locked out.
  • 8-Month Battery Life & IP65 Waterproof: Long-lasting up to 8 months with energy-efficient Zigbee. Easily replaceable AA batteries and IP65 waterproof rating ensure durability in extreme temperatures (-35°C to 66°C).

Note:
* Aqara Zigbee 3.0 hub is required.
** Calculated by opening the door 8 times a day, the batter life is 8 months.
