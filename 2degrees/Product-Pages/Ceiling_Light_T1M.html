Product Info

Description

The Aqara Ceiling Light T1M is a sleek and modern smart lighting solution designed to enhance the ambiance of any room. Featuring a minimalist design with a high-quality, durable finish, the T1M offers customizable lighting options with adjustable brightness and colour temperature. It integrates seamlessly with the Aqara ecosystem, allowing users to control the light via the Aqara Home app or voice commands through smart assistants like Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant. The light supports automation and scheduling, making it a perfect choice for creating personalized lighting scenes. Additionally, its energy-efficient LED technology ensures long-lasting performance, providing both style and functionality for any space.

Key Features

  • RGBIC Status Light Ring:Use the side light for gradient effects or as a status indicator for smart home devices, changing colours based on alerts like leaks or open doors.
  • Bright Whites & 16 million Colours:RGB CCT technology offers 16 million colours, dynamic effects, and adjustable temperatures (2700K-6500K) for optimal lighting.
  • Ra90 Lighting & Ultra Long Lifespan:High colour accuracy (Ra90) and 50,000 hours of use for vibrant, stable lighting with no distortion.
  • Compatibility & Matter Support:Works with HomeKit, Alexa, Google, IFTTT, and Matter, enabling voice control and easy integration.
  • Multi-Control & Home Automation:Compatible with Aqara Control Devices and automates lighting based on environmental changes, even during network outages.
  • Low Consumption & Easy Connection:Acts as a Zigbee mesh repeater, reducing network congestion and idle power use for better efficiency.
  • Low Consumption & Easy Connection:Acts as a Zigbee mesh repeater, reducing network congestion and idle power use for better efficiency.
  • Versatile Fit & Easy Installation:Flicker-free, eye-friendly, and simple to install in any room, perfect for various home settings.
