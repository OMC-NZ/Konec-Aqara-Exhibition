Product Info

Description

The Aqara Hub M3 is a powerful smart home controller that brings convenience, privacy, and seamless integration to your home. With no need for an internet connection or additional hubs, it operates locally to ensure your data stays secure. The hub supports dual-band Wi-Fi and PoE for flexible connectivity and features a 360° IR blaster, allowing you to control your air conditioner and other devices with ease. It also connects with Aqara products and, for the first time, third-party Matter devices, creating a unified and easy-to-manage smart home ecosystem. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or new to smart home automation, the Aqara Hub M3 offers simple setup and reliable performance.

Key Features

  • Edge Computing & Local Control: The M3 improves stability by moving automations from the cloud to local, with a “hub replacement” feature for easy upgrades.
  • Multi-Protocol & Matter Support: Connect Aqara Zigbee and Thread devices to the Matter ecosystem for seamless smart home automation.
  • IR Control with Matter: Control IR and AC devices through Matter, with instant remote adjustments reflected in the M3 app for a unified experience.
  • Superior Connectivity: Dual-band Wi-Fi, RJ45 port, PoE, and Type-C ensure fast speeds, better coverage, and reliable internet.
  • Privacy Focused: Keeps your data secure on the device, with no cloud uploads to protect your privacy.
  • Effortless Setup: Flexible power options and easy app setup make installation quick and simple for all users.
