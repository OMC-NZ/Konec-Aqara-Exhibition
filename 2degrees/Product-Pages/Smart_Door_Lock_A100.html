Product Info

Description

The Aqara Smart Door Lock A100 is a versatile and secure smart lock that integrates seamlessly with major smart home platforms, including Apple HomeKit, Google Assistant, Alexa, and Aqara Home. With multiple unlocking methods, such as fingerprint, passcode, app control, and mechanical key, it offers flexible access for every user. The A100 uses an advanced fingerprint sensor and robust encryption technology, ensuring both convenience and security. Equipped with a durable build, anti-peep keypad, and tamper alarms, this lock is designed for high security and everyday use. Its sleek, modern design complements any home decor, while battery-powered operation ensures reliability, even in the event of a power outage. The Aqara A100 offers peace of mind, letting you manage and monitor access to your home from anywhere.

Key Features

  • 18-Month Battery Life:Powered by 8 AA batteries, lasting over 1.5 years.
  • 9 Unlock Methods:Choose from fingerprint, permanent/one-time/periodic password, NFC card, mechanical key, Aqara Home app, HomeKit/Siri, or Google Assistant.
  • Quick Fingerprint Access:Fast reader in the handle for natural, instant unlocking.
  • Backup Key & Type-C Port:Use a mechanical key or charge with the hidden Type-C port if batteries run out.
  • Smart Home Integration:Supports HomeKit and Google Assistant.
  • Zigbee & Bluetooth Connectivity:Zigbee ensures stability; Bluetooth allows direct phone connection.
  • Invisible Keypad with Away Mode:Hidden keypad with Away Mode button for automations.
  • Durable Aluminium Design:Stylish, fingerprint-resistant aluminium body.
  • Remote Configuration:Manage passwords and settings remotely, ideal for rentals.
  • Remote Unlock:Unlock via Aqara Home app, perfect for in-home deliveries.
