Product Info

Description

The Aqara Camera Hub G3 is an advanced smart home camera hub that combines security, control, and connectivity. It features 2K video resolution, delivering crisp, clear footage, and a 170° wide-angle lens that provides comprehensive room coverage. Supporting Zigbee 3.0, Apple HomeKit, and 5GHz Wi-Fi, it integrates smoothly with other smart home systems. The G3 also functions as an IR universal remote, allowing control of infrared-enabled devices throughout the home. Equipped with intelligent AI-based human detection, it accurately identifies people to reduce false alerts, and its two-way audio allows for convenient communication, making it a versatile addition to any connected home.

Key Features

  • Zigbee 3.0 Support, up to 128 devices: Connect and manage all your Aqara devices with the latest, fastest, and most energy-efficient technology.
  • 360° Infrared Control: Control your IR devices from anywhere in the room with voice commands** and local automations.
  • Ethernet RJ-45 Port: Enjoy extra stability and faster response times with a wired Ethernet connection.
  • Built-in Speaker: Use as an alarm or doorbell, customizable through automations.
  • USB Power: Offers flexible installation options and optional battery backup with Micro-USB 5V 1A power support.
  • All-Round Compatibility: Seamlessly integrate with your favorite voice assistant** or smart home ecosystem via the Aqara Home app.
  • Supports all 4 Apple HomeKit native alarm modes.

Note:
* Up to 128 Aqara Zigbee child devices can be connected to the hub (repeater devices such as Smart Plug and Wall Switch (with Neutral) are required).
**Apple Siri, Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and more are supported.
