Product Info

Description

The Aqara Smart Video Doorbell G4 is a versatile video doorbell that offers both wired and battery-powered options, making installation a breeze for any home. It’s the first of its kind to support Apple’s HomeKit Secure Video, while also seamlessly streaming to Google and Alexa smart displays. With built-in facial recognition, it notifies you of visitors and can trigger personalized automations, adding an extra layer of security and convenience with minimal setup.

Key Features

  • Local AI Face Recognition: Recognizes familiar faces locally and provides instant notifications with name tags, even without internet connection. Supports custom automations for specific visitors.
  • Apple HomeKit Compatible: The first battery-powered HomeKit Secure Video doorbell, integrating securely with Apple devices for private video storage. Also works with Google, Alexa, and Yandex for seamless smart home connections.
  • Free Cloud & Local Storage: Enjoy 7 days of free cloud storage and an option for local storage with an SD card (sold separately). NAS support is coming soon.
  • Live Video & Two-Way Talk: Check your door and talk to visitors from anywhere with clear 1080p HD video and 162° wide-angle view, plus IPX3 waterproofing for outdoor use.
  • Battery & Wired Power: Choose between up to 4 months of battery life or wired setup with battery backup for reliability during outages.
  • Indoor Chime-Repeater: Includes a loud 95 dB chime with USB-C power for flexible placement, even supporting backup power with a mini-UPS or power bank.
  • Voice Changing Option: Unique voice-changing feature lets users alter their voice for privacy, with options like “uncle” or “robot” tones.
  • Do Not Disturb Mode: Customize ring time and volume, with a night mode to avoid disturbances.
  • Custom Ringtones: Upload personalized ringtones and set messages, such as “Tom is not at home,” for specific visitors.
  • Security Alerts: Configurable alerts for tampering, temperature changes, battery levels, and visitor linger times to monitor door safety.
  • Privacy Masking: Protect neighbours’ privacy by masking up to three designated areas in the live video feed, ideal for apartment residents.

Note:
A Zigbee Hub is required for automation and linking with other smart devices.
** For HomeKit control, the latest iOS or iPadOS is recommended. HomeKit Secure Video needs a supported iCloud+ plan and a HomePod mini, HomePod, Apple TV, or iPad as a home hub. Ensure software and OS are up to date.
*** iCloud or third-party cloud storage must be managed separately via the respective app.
**** Calculation based on 35 device wakeups and 6-second event recordings daily.
***** MicroSD card not included.
****** Install in shaded areas like a porch for optimal performance.
