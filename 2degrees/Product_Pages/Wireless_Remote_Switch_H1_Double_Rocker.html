Product Info

Description

The Aqara Wireless Remote Switch H1 (Double Rocker) is a versatile and user-friendly addition to your smart home setup. With its sleek design and wireless functionality, it offers effortless control over your Aqara Zigbee 3.0 devices, even during internet outages.


This compact switch supports two modes: High-Speed Click Mode for rapid response and Multi-Function Mode for up to seven customisable actions, including single, double, and long presses on either button, as well as simultaneous presses.


Installation is hassle-free—place it on a flat surface, mount it with the included adhesive, or install it in a standard 86×86 mm wall box. Powered by a long-lasting CR2450 battery, it boasts a five-year lifespan, making it both eco-friendly and cost-effective. .


Compatible with Aqara Home, Apple HomeKit, and IFTTT, this switch seamlessly integrates into your existing smart home ecosystem. Enhance your home's intelligence and convenience with the Aqara Wireless Remote Switch H1 (Double Rocker).


Key Features

  • Zigbee 3.0 Protocol*: Experience faster, more stable, and energy-efficient performance with the latest Zigbee standard—offering improved compatibility, enhanced features, and long-term support.
  • Smart Automation Ready: Works seamlessly with Aqara Home and Apple HomeKit to trigger automations, switch between scenes, and adapt functions based on time of day or other conditions.
  • Exceptional Battery Life: Enjoy up to five years of battery life—the longest of any Aqara wireless switch.
  • High-Speed Click Mode: Reduces response time to as low as 50 ms for ultra-fast control (note: double and long press functions are disabled in this mode).
  • Multiple Configurable Actions: Supports single press, double press, long press, and simultaneous button actions to suit your smart home needs.
  • Flexible Installation Options: Place it on any flat surface, stick it using the included adhesive pad, or screw it into a standard wall box—your choice.
  • Premium Build Quality: Features the same elegant design as the flagship Wall Switch H1 EU for a consistent and high-end look.
  • 2-Way Control Support: Pair with the Wall Switch H1 EU to control the same lights from multiple locations—no extra wiring required.

Note:
* A compatible Aqara Zigbee 3.0 hub (e.g. Hub M1S, Hub M2, or Camera Hub G2H) is required.
