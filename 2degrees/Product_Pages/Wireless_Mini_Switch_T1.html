Product Info

Description

Discover the compact, battery-powered smart button that transforms your smart home experience. This stylish Zigbee 3.0 switch requires no wiring and fits anywhere—simply stick it where it suits you


With one button offering a single press, double press or long press, you can trigger three different actions. Use it to control smart lights, plugs, scenes or automations. It also serves as a or an emergency alert button that triggers notifications and alarms on your Aqara hub.


Built to last, it offers up to 50,000 presses, uses a CR2032 battery, and sports a flame-retardant V-0 PC panel for safety. The T1 version adds OTA update support for improved reliability.


Once paired with an Aqara Hub, it integrates seamlessly with Apple Home/KIT, Matter, Samsung SmartThings, Yandex Smart Home, Alexa, Google Home and IFTTT.


Compact, intuitive and versatile—this button is your effortless gateway to smarter living.


Key Features

  • Designed to Fit Anywhere: No wiring needed. its sleek, minimal design means you can place it wherever you like or stick it onto any surface using the included adhesive.
  • Compatible with Multiple Ecosystems: Once connected to an Aqara hub, the sensor can be linked with third-party platforms like Apple Home and Matter. It also works natively with Samsung SmartThings and Yandex Smart Home.
  • Smart Home Automation with Aqara: Easily create custom automations and scenes using the full range of Aqara smart home products.
  • Use it as a Smart Doorbell: Pair it with an Aqara hub that has a speaker to turn the switch into a wireless doorbell. You can even personalise the ringtone through the Aqara Home app.
  • Emergency Button Functionality: The Wireless Mini Switch can serve as an emergency button. When pressed, your Aqara Hub will trigger an alarm and send a push notification for added peace of mind.
  • Built to Last with Advanced Technology: Engineered to withstand up to 50,000 presses*. Made with V-0 grade flame-retardant PC material, it’s highly resistant to heat and built for durability.
  • OTA Update Support: The Wireless Mini Switch T1 supports over-the-air updates, offering stability and enhanced performance.
