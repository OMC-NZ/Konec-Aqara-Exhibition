Product Info

Description

The Aqara Hub M100 is designed to bring convenience, speed, and flexibility to your smart home setup. With support for both Zigbee and Thread devices, it acts as a central hub to unify your smart ecosystem – creating a seamless, reliable connection between all your devices. Whether you're turning on lights, adjusting the thermostat, or automating routines, the M100 keeps everything working together effortlessly.

Equipped with Wi-Fi 6 for faster, more stable performance, the M100 ensures smooth communication across your network, even in busy homes. It’s also fully Matter-ready, meaning it can easily connect to your favourite smart platforms like Apple Home, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa – giving you more choice, and more control.


Setup is quick and simple. Powered via USB, the Hub M100 can be installed almost anywhere – from wall sockets to power strips and USB-enabled appliances. No complicated wiring, no hassle.


Whether you're just starting your smart home journey or expanding an existing setup, the M100 is the ideal upgrade. It’s compact, powerful, and built to adapt to your needs – now and into the future.


Streamline your smart home with the Aqara Hub M100 – smart, simple, and designed with you in mind.


Key Features

  • Matter Hub for Aqara and Third-Party Devices: Easily connect your Aqara Zigbee and Thread devices to Apple Home, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and more. The Hub M100 acts as both a Matter Bridge and a Matter Controller, allowing seamless integration and smart automations via the Aqara Home app. It’s a cost-effective way to bring multiple smart home systems together in one simple hub. *.
  • Thread Boarder Router & Mesh Extender: The M100 also works as a Thread Border Router, extending the range and stability of your Thread network using mesh technology. Whether you're adding more Thread-enabled devices or improving connectivity in your smart home, the M100 strengthens your setup and helps maintain a fast, reliable experience.
  • Reliable Local Automations: Enjoy fast and dependable automations that run locally, without needing an internet connection. The M100 supports local control of Aqara Zigbee and Matter Thread devices, ensuring smooth performance, even if your Wi-Fi goes down. You’ll also benefit from improved privacy and reduced latency. **
  • Advanced Matter Bridging Support: Take advantage of advanced Matter bridging to bring together Aqara-exclusive features and third-party Matter devices, including support for Home Assistant. From lights to sensors, the M100 keeps your smart home setup connected and future-proof—across ecosystems and device brands.
  • Wi-Fi 6 with Enhanced Security: With support for 2.4GHz Wi-Fi 6 and WPA3 encryption, the M100 delivers stronger, faster connections and improved security. Perfect for busy homes with multiple devices, it ensures smooth, stable performance at all times.
  • USB-A Power: Power the Hub M100 easily through any USB-A port—use a wall adapter, power bank, PC, or even your router. Its compact, flexible design makes installation quick and tidy in any room or setup.

Note:
* To use the Hub M100 with a specific Matter ecosystem, that ecosystem must have its own Matter Controller. The Hub supports up to 20 Zigbee devices, or up to 40 mixed devices (Zigbee + Thread + Wi-Fi).
** Some features like push notifications still require an internet connection.
