Product Info

Description

Enjoy the perfect balance of function and ambience with the Aqara LED Bulb T2. Whether you need bright, high-quality white light to stay focused or a soothing spectrum of colours to set the mood, this smart bulb delivers.

It’s energy-efficient, with ultra-low standby power use, and integrates seamlessly into your smart home. Built to last and adapt to your lifestyle, the Aqara LED Bulb T2 is a smart lighting choice that brings comfort and convenience to every corner of your home.

Key Features

  • Thread and Zigbee Support: The Aqara LED Bulb T2 offers flexible dual-protocol support, allowing you to switch between Zigbee and Thread based on your needs. In Zigbee mode, you’ll unlock exclusive Aqara features and Matter compatibility via an Aqara hub. In Thread mode, the bulb connects directly to Matter-enabled ecosystems such as Apple Home, Google Home, and Alexa, delivering fast, stable performance. Whether you want Aqara’s smart features or the wider compatibility of Thread, the T2 fits right into your smart home setup.
  • 16 Million Colors (RGB versions only): With a powerful brightness of up to 1100lm (450lm for GU10), the RGB CCT version of the T2 brings stunning light to your home. It offers a full colour temperature range from warm 2000K to crisp 9000K whites, plus 16 million vibrant colours to match any mood or occasion. With a high colour rendering index (CRI) of Ra>90, your spaces will be beautifully lit with rich, natural tones.
  • Wide Tunable Whites (CCT Version only): Also delivering up to 1100lm (GU10: 450lm), the CCT model provides a versatile white light range of 2700K to 6500K. Whether you’re after a warm, inviting glow or bright daylight tones, this bulb adjusts to suit your needs. Its high CRI (Ra>90) ensures colours appear vivid and true to life.
  • Circadian Lighting: Enjoy natural, adaptive lighting thanks to full compatibility with Apple HomeKit Adaptive Lighting. The T2 automatically adjusts its colour temperature throughout the day to support your body’s natural rhythm, promoting comfort and wellbeing.
  • Power Off Memory Support: Take control of what happens when power is restored. The T2 allows you to choose whether it turns back on or stays off after an outage—ideal for preventing disturbances during the night and keeping your home experience smooth and predictable.
  • Automations with Aqara and Third-Party Devices Seamlessly create custom scenes and routines using Aqara Home or third-party platforms. Automate your lighting to match your lifestyle, from waking up gently to setting the perfect movie-night mood.
  • Exclusive Features of Aqara Home: The T2 goes beyond basic smart bulbs with advanced transition settings, smooth dimming, and curve control for flawless lighting. You can also set dynamic or static effects to suit your environment. It works perfectly with Aqara smart switches too, ensuring reliable control without flickering or connectivity issues—just seamless, elegant lighting every time.

Note:
* Zigbee mode requires a compatible Aqara hub.
** Thread mode requires a Matter controller and Thread Border Router for your chosen ecosystem.
*** Some features are only available when using Zigbee mode with a compatible Aqara hub.
