Product Info

Description

The Aqara Hub M3 is a powerful smart home controller that brings convenience, privacy, and seamless integration to your home. With no need for an internet connection or additional hubs, it operates locally to ensure your data stays secure.


The hub supports dual-band Wi-Fi and PoE for flexible connectivity and features a 360° IR blaster, allowing you to control your air conditioner and other devices with ease. It also connects with Aqara products and, for the first time, third-party Matter devices, creating a unified and easy-to-manage smart home ecosystem.


Whether you're a tech enthusiast or new to smart home automation, the Aqara Hub M3 offers simple setup and reliable performance.


Key Features

  • Edge Computing for Fast, Local Automation: Edge Computing for Fast, Local Automation: The M3 shifts key automations from the cloud to local control*, improving speed, reliability, and overall smart home performance. Its hub replacement feature ensures a smooth upgrade path from older Aqara models without losing control of your setup. **
  • Multi-Protocol Support with Matter Compatibility: Simplify your smart home with the M3 Hub—your central bridge for Aqara Zigbee and Thread devices. Enjoy seamless integration into the Matter ecosystem, without worrying about protocol compatibility. ***
  • Matter-Enabled IR Controller for Smart AC Control: Matter-Enabled IR Controller for Smart AC Control: Control IR and AC thermostat devices with ease via Matter—bringing Apple Home’s first IR support. Place the M3 near your air conditioner and see real-time updates in the Aqara app, from temperature changes to mode adjustments.
  • Reliable Wired & Wireless Connectivity: Stay connected with dual-band Wi-Fi and a built-in RJ45 Ethernet port. Power the M3 via USB-C or PoE, giving you the flexibility to install it wherever it suits you best. ****
  • Privacy-Focused with Encrypted Local Storage: Your data stays yours. The M3 protects your personal information with secure on-device storage, keeping it private and out of the cloud.
  • Easy Setup, Flexible Placement: Designed for effortless installation, the M3 supports various power and network options. Whether you’re a smart home novice or expert, setting up is quick and intuitive—no matter the space. *****
  • Built-In Speaker for Alerts & Announcements: Stay informed with custom audio notifications. The M3’s speaker can play alerts or personalised ringtones—for example, sounding an alarm when motion is detected—helping you stay on top of what matters.

Note:
* Some automations (e.g. push notifications, weather, geofencing) still require cloud connectivity.
** Reconfiguration needed for automations/scenes with migrated devices. Google Home and Alexa integrations retain settings.
*** Third-party support currently in Lab phase—limited to select Philips Hue, Leviton, and Google Nest device types.
**** USB power adapter (2A) not included.
***** Optimal performance requires minimal obstacles and additional Zigbee repeaters where needed.
