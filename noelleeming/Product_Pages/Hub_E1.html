Product Info

Description

The Aqara Hub E1 is a sleek and compact smart home hub that connects your Aqara devices to a central control point.


With its easy setup and seamless integration, it enhances your smart home experience by enabling automation and remote control of devices via the Aqara Home app.


Featuring a stable Zigbee connection, it supports a wide range of Aqara sensors, lights, and other smart devices, making it the perfect foundation for building a smart, connected home.


Key Features

  • Zigbee 3.0 Hub for 128 Devices: Fast, stable, and energy-efficient, with long-term support and enhanced features.
  • All-Round Compatibility: Works with popular ecosystems and voice assistants like HomeKit, Alexa, Google, IFTTT, and more.
  • Powered by USB-A: Can be powered via any USB-equipped device (e.g., power bank, router, PC).
  • 210° Adjustable Shaft: Flexible placement with a fully adjustable shaft.
  • Wi-Fi Repeater: Expands Zigbee network and boosts Wi-Fi coverage for Aqara and other devices.
  • USB Dongle Mode: Turns into a Zigbee USB dongle for developers (B2B only, MOQ required).AI Facial & Gesture Recognition: Recognizes faces and gestures for personalized notifications and automations
