Product Info

Description

The Aqara Temperature and Humidity Sensor T1 is a compact and reliable smart device designed to monitor environmental conditions in real-time. It measures both temperature and humidity with high accuracy, providing users with insights into the indoor climate of their homes or offices.

The sensor connects seamlessly to the Aqara ecosystem, allowing integration with other smart devices for automated responses, such as adjusting heating, ventilation, or air conditioning (HVAC) systems. With a long battery life and easy installation via a magnetic mount, the Aqara Temperature and Humidity Sensor T1 is ideal for improving comfort and energy efficiency.


It is also compatible with popular smart home platforms, including Apple HomeKit, Amazon Alexa, and Google Assistant.


Key Features

  • 2-Year Battery Life: A single small battery powers the sensor for up to two years or more, giving you long-lasting peace of mind.
  • Detailed Data Logging: Real-time tracking with cloud recording lets you review historical temperature, humidity, and pressure data at any time—no subscription required..
  • Custom Notifications: Get instant alerts via the Aqara Home app when your set temperature, humidity, or pressure thresholds are reached.
  • Seamless Multi-Ecosystem Support: Once connected to an Aqara Hub, the sensor works with Apple Home, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Matter. It also integrates natively with Samsung SmartThings and Yandex Smart Home.
  • Smarter Automations with Aqara: Combine with other Aqara devices for tailored scenes—for example, automatically switching on a bathroom exhaust fan when humidity rises during a shower.
  • High-Precision Technology: Built with an industrial-grade Sensirion sensor, offering accuracy of ±3% for humidity and ±0.3°C for temperature.
  • Enhanced with OTA Updates: The latest T1 series supports over-the-air updates, improving both stability and functionality over time.

Note:
* Requires pairing with a smart plug or IR unit.
