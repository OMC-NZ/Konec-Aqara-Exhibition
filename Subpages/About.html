About Aqara

Aqara is a combination of the Latin words “Acutulus” (smart) and “Ara” (home).

At Aqara, we're committed to creating interconnected, personalized smart home experiences that are accessible to everyone. As a global leader and pioneer in IoT, we offer one of the widest selections of products in more than 30 categories, serving over 12 million users worldwide.

Our products blend cutting-edge hardware technology, sophisticated software design, and AI capabilities and have been recognized with over 300 industry accolades. Designed to integrate seamlessly with major third-party ecosystems, offering users unparalleled flexibility. Aqara’s solutions are tailored to provide the ultimate smart home experience by making homes smarter, more sustainable, and adaptable to users’ evolving needs.

Since it's founding in 2016, Aqara has continued to expand its global footprint and we aspire to bring smart home technology to every household around the world.

This site is managed by an Aqara authorised distributor in New Zealand. If there are any queries or questions, please feel free to contact us at aqarahome@konec.co.nz

Wide Compatibility

Aqara Smart Home products are widely compatible and can work seamlessly with the smart devices you already use every day. They support all major platforms including Matter, Apple HomeKit, Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa.
