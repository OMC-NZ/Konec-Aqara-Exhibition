About Aqara

Aqara comes from the Latin words “Acutulus” (smart) and “Ara” (home) - because our goal is simple: to make every home a truly smart home.

Around the world, Aqara is a leading smart home and IoT brand, with products across more than 30 categories and over 12 million users. Our devices combine reliable hardware, intuitive software and powerful AI, and have received more than 300 industry awards along the way.

Our products blend cutting-edge hardware technology, sophisticated software design, and AI capabilities and have been recognized with over 300 industry accolades. Designed to integrate seamlessly with major third-party ecosystems, offering users unparalleled flexibility. Aqara's solutions are tailored to provide the ultimate smart home experience by making homes smarter, more sustainable, and adaptable to users' evolving needs.

Everything is designed to work together and with the platforms you already use, so you don't have to start from scratch. Aqara makes homes smarter, more efficient and more comfortable, while adapting to the way you actually live.
