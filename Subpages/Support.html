Help & Support

Need help with your Aqara device?

Refer to your device's FAQ, via:

  • Your Aqara Home App:
    Navigate to Profile > Help & Feedback > Select your device type > Select your device.
  • Product List:
    Find your device on the "Products page", then review the support documents located at the bottom of the page.

Experiencing an issue?

If you are experiencing an issue with your Aqara device, please follow the below steps:

  • Step 1:
    Refer to your device FAQ for detailed troubleshooting and resolution steps to address the most common issues.
  • Step 2:
    If further assistance is needed, submit a support ticket to our Aqara Technical Support. Our team will troubleshoot and help to resolve the problem.
