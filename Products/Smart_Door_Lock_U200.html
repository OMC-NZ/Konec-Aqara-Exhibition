Product Info

Description

Experience a smarter, more convenient way to secure your home with the Aqara Smart Lock U200 Kit. Designed for easy installation, the U200 fits seamlessly over your existing lock with no drilling required, making it the perfect upgrade for modern living. Enjoy a range of secure entry options, including fingerprint recognition, NFC, passcodes, Apple Home Keys, and more — giving you flexibility and peace of mind.


Built with Matter compatibility, the U200 integrates effortlessly with your smart home and can be easily managed via the Aqara Home app for full control at your fingertips. With an impressive six-month battery life and a durable IPX5 waterproof rating, this lock is designed to perform reliably in both indoor and outdoor environments.


Whether you’re looking for enhanced security, convenience, or seamless integration with your smart home, the Aqara Smart Lock U200 offers it all — making keyless living simple, secure, and stylish.


Key Features

  • Apple Home Key & Multiple Unlocking Methods: The Aqara Smart Lock U200 offers a wide range of unlocking options, including Apple Home Keys (requires an Apple 2-in-1 Matter Controller & Border Router), fingerprint**, passcode, NFC*, mechanical keys, and more. Control your lock through the Aqara Home app, using integrated Bluetooth for local access or Thread for secure remote management. Temporary one-time and periodic passwords make sharing access simple and flexible.
  • Smart Features & Control: Manage your lock with ease using the Aqara Home app, giving you access to Quiet Unlock Mode, automatic locking, activity logs, and real-time alerts. The U200 also features Anti-Peep Pin Code Protection**** and offers flexible options like Passage Mode, Do Not Disturb Mode, and offline passwords, ensuring full control at your fingertips.
  • Seamless Smart Home Integration: With native Matter support over Thread, the U200 works effortlessly with major platforms, including Apple Home and Google Home, providing smooth control and extended battery life. Remote management is secure, simple, and efficient, keeping your home connected and protected. *****
  • Long-Lasting Power & Durability: Equipped with a rechargeable Li-Ion battery lasting up to six months******, the U200 also supports AAA batteries******* and optional hard-wired power. Built with an IPX5 waterproof rating and designed to operate in temperatures from -15°C to 66°C, it’s perfect for both indoor and outdoor use, offering reliable performance all year round.
  • Easy Installation: Upgrade your door in minutes with the U200’s quick, no-drill installation. It’s designed to fit over your existing lock and is compatible with Euro profiles and selected Scandinavian cylinders. Simply check your door compatibility******** before purchasing to ensure a perfect fit
  • Enhanced Security: The U200 is built with AES encryption and fingerprint module encryption binding, ensuring your personal data and access remain secure at all times. With advanced encryption standards, you can trust the U200 to deliver peace of mind for your home and family.

Note:
* NFC cards only available in certain models (Smart Lock U200 Kit).
** Fingerprints can be configured locally on the lock by pressing the confirm button for 3 seconds using the Admin's password.
*** Settings can only be adjusted via direct Bluetooth connection.
**** Anti-peep pin code protection allows random digits to be entered before and after the actual code for enhanced security.
***** Upon release, U200 will support integration with Apple Home, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa Matter controllers. Compatibility with Samsung SmartThings controllers will follow.
****** Battery life is based on up to 8 daily locking and unlocking actions via fingerprint/NFC.
******* AAA batteries are not rechargeable; hardwired installation requires a 12V-24V AC or DC power supply.
******** The U200 is compatible with locking cylinders that allow key access from outside, even when a key is already inserted inside.
