Product Info

Description

The Aqara Wireless Mini Switch T1 is a compact, battery-powered smart button designed to offer versatile control within a smart home ecosystem. Compatible with Zigbee 3.0, this switch can be paired with Aqara hubs and is highly configurable to control various smart devices, from lighting to appliances. Its minimalistic design includes a single button capable of multiple actions, including single press, double press, and long press, allowing users to perform up to three distinct functions with one device. The switch is easy to install, requires no wiring, and can be placed virtually anywhere within reach of the connected hub, making it an ideal solution for flexible and efficient smart home control.

Key Features

  • Versatile Placement: No wiring needed. Compact design sticks or places anywhere.
  • Apple Home Integration: Control HomeKit-enabled accessories through the Apple Home app.
  • Seamless Automation: Customize scenes with Aqara's suite of smart home products.
  • Smart Doorbell Option: Use as a doorbell with Aqara Hub, with customizable ringtones.
  • Emergency Button: Acts as an alert button, triggering push notifications and alarms.
  • Built to Last: Supports up to 50,000 presses with a flame-retardant, heat-resistant design.
