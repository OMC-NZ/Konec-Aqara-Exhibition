Product Info

Description

The Aqara Cube T1 Pro is a powerful and intuitive smart wireless controller designed to give you effortless control over your smart home.


Building on the charm of the original Cube, the T1 Pro brings a fresh and simplified experience—no need to remember multiple gestures. Instead, each of its six sides can be assigned to control different scenes or devices, making it easier than ever to interact with your home.


Whether you're turning off the lights, activating a movie mode, or adjusting the temperature, the Cube T1 Pro puts seamless control in the palm of your hand.


Key Features

  • Zigbee 3.0 Built-In: Experience lightning-fast, ultra-reliable performance with Zigbee 3.0—the gold standard for smart home tech. Enjoy better compatibility, longer support, and smarter energy use, all in one powerful protocol
  • Supercharged Compatibility: Whether you're team Apple, Amazon, or somewhere in between, the Cube T1 Pro has you covered. With the new Scene Mode, it's now HomeKit-ready and prepped for Matter* once it’s fully rolled out. It also plays nicely with Alexa, IFTTT, and more!
  • Smarter Scene Control: Get creative with Scene Mode: assign each cube face to a different device or scene! Clear dice-style markers help you instantly recognise which face does what—making control effortless and fun.
  • Choose your mode: Switch to Action Mode to recreate the classic gestures—now powered by Zigbee 3.0 for faster, smarter performance.
  • Battery That Lasts for Years: No more constant battery swaps. With its ultra-low energy design, the Cube T1 Pro keeps going for up to 2 years—eco-friendly and worry-free.
  • Twist to Control*: A simple twist of the cube lets you fine-tune your lights or curtains. Adjust brightness, temperature, or even curtain position with a spin—customise everything in the Aqara Home app.
  • Always Getting Smarter: Stay ahead with over-the-air (OTA) updates. New features, improvements, and fixes come straight to your cube—no need to buy the next model.

Note:
* Compatible Aqara devices are required.
