Product Info

Description

The Aqara Temperature and Humidity Sensor T1 is a compact and reliable smart device designed to monitor environmental conditions in real-time. It measures both temperature and humidity with high accuracy, providing users with insights into the indoor climate of their homes or offices. The sensor connects seamlessly to the Aqara ecosystem, allowing integration with other smart devices for automated responses, such as adjusting heating, ventilation, or air conditioning (HVAC) systems. With a long battery life and easy installation via a magnetic mount, the Aqara Temperature and Humidity Sensor T1 is ideal for improving comfort and energy efficiency. It is also compatible with popular smart home platforms, including Apple HomeKit, Amazon Alexa, and Google Assistant.

Key Features

  • 2-Year Battery Life: Long-lasting battery, providing up to 2 years of operation.
  • Data Logging: Real-time tracking and cloud storage of historical data, no subscription required.
  • Notifications: Get alerts in the Aqara Home app when temperature, humidity, or pressure exceed preset limits.
  • Multi-Ecosystem Support: Compatible with Aqara Hub and third-party platforms like Apple Home, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Matter.
  • Aqara Home Automations: Trigger smart scenes, such as turning on a bathroom exhaust when humidity rises.
  • Advanced Technology: Industrial-grade sensor with ±3% humidity and ±0.3°C temperature accuracy

Note:
* Requires pairing with a smart plug or IR unit.
