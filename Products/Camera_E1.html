Product Info

Description

The Aqara Camera E1 is an affordable smart home camera designed for effortless security. With 2K resolution, 360° pan-and-tilt, and night vision, it captures clear, wide-angle footage while monitoring your home in real time. Its AI-based person detection reduces false alerts and enables smart automations, such as turning on lights when motion is detected.


Fully compatible with Apple HomeKit Secure Video, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa, the E1 integrates seamlessly into your smart home ecosystem. Privacy features include customisable rotation angles, microphone control, and WPA3 encryption, giving you peace of mind.


Flexible storage options support microSD cards up to 512GB and cloud storage. Installation is simple with Bluetooth 5.2 and Wi-Fi 6 support, plus versatile mounting options. The Aqara Camera E1 is a reliable, secure, and future-proof choice for modern home surveillance.


Key Features

  • Compatible with Apple Home, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa: The Camera E1 works seamlessly with popular smart home ecosystems, including Apple Home, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa. It supports Apple HomeKit Secure Video, ensuring your recordings are privately analysed and encrypted on devices like Apple TV or HomePod, with secure iCloud storage*. With an iCloud+ subscription, you can access up to 10 days of activity from one or more cameras.
  • Crystal-Clear 2K HD with Pan & Tilt: Enjoy stunning 2K ultra-high-definition video and monitor real-time events effortlessly. Keep an eye on your children, check if anyone needs help, or simply stay informed about your home. The camera features two-way audio, night vision, and a 360° pan-and-tilt function, making it easy to track and record activity across large spaces.
  • Enhanced Privacy Protection: The E1 prioritises your privacy. Activate privacy mode to rotate the camera to a specific position, disable it, or hide selected areas from view. For the first time, you can also turn off audio recording or the microphone manually or via automation. With WPA3 support, your data is more secure, reducing potential vulnerabilities.
  • 24/7 Recording & Flexible Storage: Record around the clock for peace of mind. Insert a Micro SD card (not included) for continuous recording or choose cloud storage with one day free***. Multiple local and cloud storage options give you flexibility, so you can store videos your way without limits.
  • Smart Person Detection & Automation: E1 uses AI to detect human shapes and perform 350° tracking. Integrate it with other Aqara devices for smart automations. For instance, lights can switch on when the camera detects someone in the hallway, or the camera can turn to a specific position when front door sensors are triggered.
  • Easy Installation & Reliable Connection: Set up is simple thanks to Bluetooth MagicPair technology and Wi-Fi 6 certification. The camera can be installed horizontally or inverted using the 1/4-inch threaded screw and base, and it uses a USB-C power interface for convenience.
  • Customisable Features: Set up to 50 favourite location presets, create 15-second time-lapse videos, and upload custom ringtones. Power-off memory ensures you never miss important family moments, with automatic return after a power outage.
