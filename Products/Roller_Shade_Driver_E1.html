Product Info

Description

The Aqara Roller Shade Driver E1 is a smart home device designed to automate window treatments effortlessly. This compact and sleek driver is compatible with most standard roller shades, allowing you to control them remotely through a smartphone app or via voice commands with smart assistants like Apple HomeKit, Amazon Alexa, and Google Assistant.


Key features include smart control for easy operation from anywhere, scheduling options to automatically open or close shades at specific times, and reliable wireless connectivity via Zigbee, which conserves energy. The driver is designed for straightforward installation on existing roller shades without the need for complex tools or professional help, and its battery-powered design offers flexibility in placement without requiring hardwiring.


With its unobtrusive and stylish appearance, the Aqara Roller Shade Driver E1 enhances your home's comfort and convenience, making it an ideal addition to any smart home setup.


Key Features

  • Easy Setup: Mount to the wall, attach the adapter, and insert the beaded cord—quick and simple.
  • Zigbee 3.0: Fast, stable, and energy-efficient connection with advanced compatibility.
  • Wide Compatibility: Works with HomeKit, Alexa, Google, IFTTT, and more.
  • Custom Settings: Pre-set open/close percentages for personalized light control.
  • Long Battery Life: Up to 2 months on a single charge.
  • USB Type-C: Charge or use continuous power with the versatile USB-C port.
  • One-Button Control: Operate via Aqara app or Mini Switch.
  • Smart Automations: Set up automations for independent operation.
  • 4 Cord Adapters: Includes adapters for various beaded cords.
