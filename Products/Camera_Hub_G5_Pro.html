Product Info

Description

Take your outdoor security to the next level with the Camera Hub G5 Pro. Offering exceptional image quality and advanced AI recognition, it’s built to elevate your smart home experience.


With an integrated Zigbee hub, Matter Controller, and Thread Border Router, the G5 Pro acts as a powerful and versatile hub for your connected devices. Available in both Wi-Fi and Power over Ethernet (PoE) models, it provides reliable performance and flexible installation options to suit your needs.


Key Features

  • First Aqara Outdoor Camera with Zigbee, Thread & Matter: The G5 Pro is a powerful smart home hub, acting as a Matter Controller, Bridge, and Thread Border Router. It supports both Aqara and third-party Matter devices, enabling seamless local automations and integration with Apple Home, Google Home, and Alexa. It also functions as a mesh repeater to enhance network coverage.
  • Smart AI Detection: Visual & Audio: Equipped with a built-in Neural Processing Unit (NPU), the G5 Pro can detect people, vehicles, faces, and lingering behaviour, alongside recognising sounds like crying, barking, alarms, and coughing. It processes everything locally—even without internet—for faster, more secure automation and alerts.
  • True Full-Color Night Vision: Featuring a graphene-cooled 1/1.8" sensor and f/1.0 aperture lens, the G5 Pro captures vivid, high-definition 2.6K images even in low light. Its built-in spotlight enhances clarity and acts as a visual deterrent, offering unmatched night-time protection.
  • HomeKit Secure Video & End-to-End Encryption: Enjoy peace of mind with full Apple HomeKit Secure Video support. Footage is analysed locally on your Apple TV or HomePod and encrypted end-to-end, never leaving your home. Store recordings locally or back them up to NAS, iCloud, or Aqara Cloud.
  • Dual-Band Stability (Wi-Fi Only): The Wi-Fi model features both 2.4GHz and 5GHz support with WPA3 encryption. It connects via a 5V/2A USB-C cable, giving users freedom to choose cable lengths for their ideal setup..
  • Wired for Reliability (PoE Only): The PoE model supports Ethernet for data and power via an RJ45 port—ideal for existing installations needing rock-solid connectivity. It also includes a 5V/2A USB-C option for added setup flexibility.
  • Local Storage and RTSP Compatibility: Includes 32GB encrypted eMMC storage (non-removable), with NAS expansion and RTSP support. Easily stream via Home Assistant, NVRs, or VLC. Works with Alexa, Google, and SmartThings for a fully integrated home.
  • Flexible Mounting & Streaming Options: Supports RTSP for low-latency local streaming. Easily install it on walls, ceilings, or flat surfaces using its rotatable stand or standard ¼-inch thread.
  • Built for Harsh Conditions: With an IP65 weatherproof rating, the G5 Pro performs in rain, snow, dust, or extreme cold—down to -30°C—ensuring year-round protection indoors or out.
  • Customisable Outdoor Security: Two-way talk with a 100 dB speaker and sensitive microphone lets you communicate clearly with visitors or deter threats. Features like Privacy Masking, Correlated Events, and custom alerts offer advanced, tailored monitoring via the Aqara Home app.
