Product Info

Description

The Aqara Smart Video Doorbell G4 is an advanced, versatile video doorbell that combines security with smart home integration. It features a high-resolution 1080p camera with a 162° wide-angle lens, providing a clear, panoramic view of your entryway.

The Smart Video Doorbell G4 is compatible with Apple HomeKit Secure Video, Google Assistant, Alexa, and IFTTT, allowing seamless integration with various smart home ecosystems. It supports both local and cloud storage options, with video storage available via an SD card or free seven-day cloud storage.

The doorbell includes customizable ringtones, facial recognition, and motion detection, ensuring that you receive alerts only when it detects familiar or unknown faces. Its weather-resistant design and extended battery life offer reliable outdoor performance, while the option for wired or battery-powered installation adds flexibility for any home setup.

Key Features

  • Local AI Face Recognition: Recognizes familiar faces locally and provides instant notifications with name tags, even without internet connection. Supports custom automations for specific visitors.
  • Widely Compatible: The first battery-powered HomeKit Secure Video doorbell, integrating securely with Apple devices for private video storage. Also works with Google, Alexa, and Yandex for seamless smart home connections.
  • Free Cloud & Local Storage: Enjoy 7 days of free cloud storage and an option for local storage with an SD card (sold separately). NAS support is coming soon.
  • Live Video & Two-Way Talk: Check your door and talk to visitors from anywhere with clear 1080p HD video and 162° wide-angle view, plus IPX3 waterproofing for outdoor use.
  • Battery & Wired Power: Choose between up to 4 months of battery life or wired setup with battery backup for reliability during outages.
  • Indoor Chime-Repeater: Includes a loud 95 dB chime with USB-C power for flexible placement, even supporting backup power with a mini-UPS or power bank.
  • Voice Changing Option: Unique voice-changing feature lets users alter their voice for privacy, with options like “uncle” or “robot” tones.
  • Do Not Disturb Mode: Customize ring time and volume, with a night mode to avoid disturbances.
  • Custom Ringtones: Upload personalized ringtones and set messages, such as “Tom is not at home,” for specific visitors.
  • Security Alerts: Configurable alerts for tampering, temperature changes, battery levels, and visitor linger times to monitor door safety.
  • Privacy Masking: Protect neighbours’ privacy by masking up to three designated areas in the live video feed, ideal for apartment residents.

Create a hands-free doorbell experience by setting up an automation to play a pre-recorded voice message through the G4's main unit when a visitor is detected—such as: “Dear guest, please remain for 5 seconds and the doorbell will ring automatically.” After the short delay, the G4 will ring the bell without the guest needing to press anything.
