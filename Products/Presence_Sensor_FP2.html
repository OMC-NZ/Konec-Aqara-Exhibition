Product Info

Description

The Aqara Presence Sensor FP2 is an advanced motion and presence detection device designed for smart home environments. Equipped with millimetre-wave radar technology, it provides highly accurate and precise monitoring, detecting even subtle movements within its range. The sensor can differentiate between human presence and other motion, offering enhanced reliability in applications such as lighting control, security systems, and automation.


It features a wide detection range and can be integrated with other Aqara smart home products via the Aqara Hub, supporting automation and remote control through the Aqara Home app. The FP2 is ideal for use in rooms, corridors, and entrances, enhancing the efficiency and intelligence of smart home setups.


Key Features

  • Smart Zone Positioning: Monitor rooms up to ~40 m² (430 sq ft) with a single radar sensor. Divide the space into up to 30 zones—like sofa, desk, or bed—and set unique automations for each. It’s like having multiple PIR motion sensors in one device.
  • Multi-person Tracking: Detect up to 5 people at once, enabling personalised automations for each person without interference. Ideal for shared spaces like living rooms and offices.
  • Ultra-Precise & Private: Millimetre-wave radar ensures stable and highly accurate detection—even when sitting or sleeping. No cameras, no images collected, just discreet, non-intrusive sensing.
  • Seamless Smart Home Integration: Works natively with Apple Home, Google Home, Alexa, and Home Assistant—without requiring a hub. Perfect for smart home enthusiasts who want powerful automation flexibility.
  • Fall Detection & Alerts: Detects falls with >98% accuracy and sends instant alerts. A safe, private alternative to wearables or cameras. (Recommended for ceiling installation at 2.5–2.8 m height, with a detection radius of 2 m.)
  • Built-in Light Sensor: Adds brightness detection for advanced lighting automations, such as turning on lights at dusk or dimming when natural light is sufficient.
  • Reliable Local Automations: Once set up, automations run locally—even if Wi-Fi is unstable—keeping your home secure and private at all times.
  • Flexible & Durable Design: IPX5 water resistance makes it suitable for bathrooms or kitchens. With a hidden USB-C port, you can use different cables for maximum installation flexibility.

Note:
* Recommended open detection space: 10–40 m².
** Best results achieved when tracking up to 3 people.
*** Fall detection works only with ceiling installation and cannot replace medical equipment. It may not detect all types of falls (e.g., slow slides or fainting while seated).
**** Bluetooth is reserved for future Matter support.
