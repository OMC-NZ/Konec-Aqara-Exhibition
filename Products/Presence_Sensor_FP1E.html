Product Info

Description

Transform your home into a truly smart space with the Aqara Presence Sensor FP1E. Powered by mmWave Radar technology and AI, it delivers unmatched accuracy in detecting presence—even subtle movements. With its one-minute setup and seamless integration through an Aqara Hub, you’ll unlock instant, reliable automations without the hassle of complex configurations.


Enjoy greater comfort, convenience, and peace of mind every day.


Key Features

  • Ultra-Accurate mmWave Radar: Go beyond traditional motion sensors with next-gen mmWave Radar. The FP1E detects even the slightest movements in just 200 ms, providing fast and reliable response. With a wide 120° adjustable field of view, it covers up to 50 m² (ceiling-mounted) or 35 m² (wall-mounted), making it perfect for rooms of all shapes and sizes.
  • Enhanced Precision with AI: Say goodbye to false alarms. The FP1E uses AI-powered filtering to ignore everyday interferences like curtains, fans, and even small pets. Its adaptive sensitivity means it automatically adjusts to your environment, ensuring highly accurate detection that you can always rely on.
  • Hassle-Free Setup: From installation to operation in just one minute—no technical expertise required. The FP1E is designed to be intuitive and beginner-friendly, with no need to fine-tune sensitivity or manage interference. Simply install, connect, and enjoy smarter living.
  • Versatile Mounting Options: Easily install the FP1E on walls, ceilings, or corners to fit your space. Combined with its rotatable head and AI detection, you’ll enjoy reliable coverage in any room, without blind spots or interference.
  • Detects Movement & Stillness: The FP1E doesn’t just sense motion—it can also detect when someone is sitting still. This unlocks smart automations such as: sending reminders after long periods of inactivity (promoting healthier habits) and adjusting air conditioning during active gatherings like parties.
  • Multi-Ecosystem Support with Matter: With an Aqara Zigbee 3.0 Hub (sold separately, except Camera Hub G2H Pro), the FP1E supports Matter-over-bridge for seamless integration across leading platforms: Apple Home, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Samsung SmartThings, Home Assistant, IFTTT and Homey.

Note:
* When the device is initially used, occasional false detections caused by interferences might occur. However, AI Spatial Learning capabilities will continue to improve automatically over time. ** This is an Aqara Home app exclusive feature. *** Matter over bridge support requires a Matter controller for the corresponding ecosystem.
