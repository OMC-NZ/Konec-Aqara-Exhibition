Product Info

Description

The Aqara Dual Relay Module T2 brings smart control to your everyday devices. This versatile module allows you to power on and off a range of equipment—from lights and switches to garage doors and motors—instantly turning standard setups into intelligent systems. Compatible with Apple Home and other leading smart platforms, it offers effortless control right from your smartphone. Set schedules, monitor power use, and enjoy hands-free convenience with app-based remote access. Ideal for those looking to easily add smart functionality to their home, the Aqara Dual Relay Module T2 is the perfect upgrade for a connected lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Versatile Smart Control: Install the T2 in ceiling lights, wall switches, and sockets for remote control, or automate coffee machines, air fresheners, heaters, blinds, and garage doors.
  • Broad Compatibility: Works with Apple Home, Matter, Google Home, and Alexa, making it easy to integrate and automate traditional devices with other smart home products.
  • Motor Control & Interlock: Safely connect to bidirectional motors for controlling blinds, roller shades, and more.
  • Energy Monitoring: Tracks power use and retains on/off status during power outages for added convenience.
  • Safety Features: Adjustable voltage, current, and power limits ensure safe DIY installation, automatically shutting off if abnormal activity is detected.
  • Wireless Switch Mode: Convert existing wall switches into controllers for scenes, security, or lighting.
  • Easy Installation: Compact DIN rail-mounted design fits standard and US wall boxes for quick setup in tight spaces
