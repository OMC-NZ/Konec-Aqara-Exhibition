Product Info

Description

The Aqara Dual Relay Module T2 brings smart control to your everyday devices. This versatile module allows you to power on and off a range of equipment—from lights and switches to garage doors and motors—instantly turning standard setups into intelligent systems.


Compatible with Apple Home and other leading smart platforms, it offers effortless control right from your smartphone. Set schedules, monitor power use, and enjoy hands-free convenience with app-based remote access.


Ideal for those looking to easily add smart functionality to their home, the Aqara Dual Relay Module T2 is the perfect upgrade for a connected lifestyle.


Key Features

  • A Smart and Versatile Home Solution: The Dual Relay Module T2 is a truly flexible device, ideal for a wide range of home automation needs. It can be installed in ceiling lights for smart control or fitted into standard wall switches and sockets for convenient remote access. It’s also perfect for automating everyday appliances such as coffee machines, air fresheners, electric heaters, roller blinds, and even garage roller doors.
  • Compatible with Apple Home and Matter via Bridge*: The T2 works seamlessly with Apple Home, Matter, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa (via Zigbee bridge), transforming traditional devices into smart ones. It integrates easily with third-party systems, enabling automation and effortless control across your smart home.
  • Reliable Motor Control with Interlock Function: Easily and safely connect the T2 to bidirectional motors. It’s ideal for controlling tubular motors, roller blinds, or even serving as the central component in a smart motorised system.
  • Energy Monitoring & Power Memory: The T2 includes built-in energy monitoring and provides real-time power usage statistics. It also features a power memory function, restoring the last on/off state in the event of a power cut.
  • Built-In Safety and Protection: Your safety is our top priority. The T2 offers advanced protection features, including adjustable limits for voltage, current, and power. If unusual conditions are detected, it will automatically cut off the power to prevent damage or hazards. **
  • Wireless Switch Mode: Convert your existing wired wall switches into smart scene controllers. With this mode, you can use your traditional switch to arm/disarm your security system, operate curtains, or control lighting—all without needing to replace the switch. ***
  • Compact and Easy to Install: Designed for convenience: the T2 fits easily onto a DIN rail in your electrical cabinet or into standard wall boxes, including 86 mm and US-style enclosures. Its compact size makes installation quick and simple. ****

Note:
* Apple Home, Google Home, Alexa, and Matter support is available via a Zigbee bridge.
** Installation should be carried out by users experienced with electrical systems. We strongly recommend consulting a qualified electrician and using this guide as a reference only.
*** Any switch or sensor connected to the S1/S2/COM terminals must support the voltage provided to the relay.
**** The T2 supports a maximum current of 10A. A wall box depth of approximately 60 mm is recommended. If your existing box isn’t deep enough, consider using a Wireless Remote Switch H₁ or an ultra-thin surface-mounted wall switch.
