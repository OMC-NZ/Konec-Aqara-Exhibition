Product Info

Description

The Aqara Camera Hub G3 is an advanced smart home camera hub that combines security, control, and connectivity.


It features 2K video resolution, delivering crisp, clear footage, and a 170° wide-angle lens that provides comprehensive room coverage. Supporting Zigbee 3.0, Apple HomeKit, and 5GHz Wi-Fi, it integrates smoothly with other smart home systems.


The G3 also functions as an IR universal remote, allowing control of infrared-enabled devices throughout the home. Equipped with intelligent AI-based human detection, it accurately identifies people to reduce false alerts, and its two-way audio allows for convenient communication, making it a versatile addition to any connected home.


Key Features

  • AI Facial & Gesture Recognition: Recognizes faces and gestures for personalized notifications and automations
  • Zigbee 3.0 Hub: Connect up to 128 Aqara devices for home automation and remote control.
  • Local IR Control: Control existing IR-compatible devices, with local automations even offline.
  • Wide Compatibility: Supports HomeKit, Alexa, Google Home, and smart display streaming.
  • 360° Pan & Tilt: 110° lens with pan/tilt motors for up to 340° rotation, plus face and pet tracking
  • 2K 1296p Resolution: High-res 2304 x 1296 sensor for detailed images.
  • Dual-Band Wi-Fi: Supports both 2.4 and 5 GHz Wi-Fi.
  • Type-C Port: Modern port with low-latency, HD video out.
  • Privacy Focused: Hardware shutter with automatic family recognition.
  • IR Night Vision: 940 nm LEDs for clear night vision without glow.
  • Universal Mount: 1/4" screw thread for versatile mounting options.
  • One-Button Video Call: Quick emergency video message feature.

Note:
* The feature may be unavailable or partly available in third-party integrations such as HomeKit Secure Video.
** In HomeKit Secure Video mode, the camera records in Full HD 1080p due to the current limitations of HomeKit technology.
