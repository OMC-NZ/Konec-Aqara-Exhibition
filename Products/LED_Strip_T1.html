Product Info

Description

The Aqara LED Strip T1 is a versatile and energy-efficient smart lighting solution designed to enhance any space with customizable lighting effects.


It offers adjustable white temperature, from warm to cool, and vibrant RGB colours, making it perfect for creating personalized ambiance. Compatible with major smart home ecosystems like Apple HomeKit, Google Assistant, and Amazon Alexa, it allows for voice control and automation. The strip can be controlled remotely via the Aqara Home app and integrates seamlessly into smart home routines with features like scheduling, timers, and scene customization. With its easy installation and low power consumption, the Aqara LED Strip T1 is ideal for both accent lighting and functional illumination.


Key Features

  • Compatibility & Matter Support: T1 works with various ecosystems and supports Matter through the hub, ensuring easy integration and future-proofing your smart home.
  • 16 million Colours & Tuneable Whites: Enjoy a wide range of colours and adjustable white light from cool to warm to create the perfect ambiance.
  • RGBIC with Gradient Effects: Customize each of the 10 segments with different colours, dimming, and effects, allowing you to create unique lighting in different areas of the room.
  • Home Automations & Scenes: The Aqara Home app lets you customize scenes, colours, brightness, and effects for dynamic lighting control.
  • Adjustable Length & Easy Installation: Cuttable in 20 cm segments, extendable up to 10 meters, and reattachable; also rated IP44 for dust protection and outdoor use.
  • Zigbee 3.0 with Repeater Functionality: T1 enhances your Zigbee network as a repeater, ensuring reliable connections with low power consumption.

Note:
* IP44 rating refers to the LED strip only. The manual controller and power supply are not weatherproof and should be installed indoors.

Scene: Status Light for Alerts and Automations:

When the Camera Hub G3 detects an unknown face while you're away, the LED Strip T1 blinks red, the Alert System triggers the siren, and you receive a remote notification. The LED Strip T1 also shows a steady red light to indicate the Alert System is armed, flashes in case of emergencies, or signals tasks like completed laundry.

Scene: Circadian Lighting with Aqara Home Dynamic Scenes:

With Aqara Home dynamic scenes, the LED Strip T1 gradually adjusts its colour temperature from warm to cool at sunrise, creating a natural lighting transition. This feature can be used instead of HomeKit Adaptive Lighting.

Scene: Dynamic Staircase Welcome Scene:

The LED Strip T1, with addressable LED technology, can light up each step of your staircase sequentially when motion is detected. This creates a welcoming dynamic lighting effect on the stairs.

Scene: Countdown Scene for Automations:

When the door is open while the Alert System is armed, the LED Strip T1 starts a countdown, showing the time remaining to disarm the system. This feature can be set up with an Aqara Home Alert System delay, like a 10-second countdown.
