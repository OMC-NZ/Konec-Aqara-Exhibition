Product Info

Description

The Aqara Smart Lock U50 is a versatile, secure smart lock designed for easy access and integration into modern smart homes. It offers multiple unlocking methods, including keypad entry, NFC card (sold separately), physical key, and Apple Home Key, which allows users to tap their iPhone or Apple Watch to unlock, even if the device is out of power.


Compatible with Apple HomeKit, Google Assistant, Alexa, and IFTTT, the U50 enables seamless automation and remote access through the Aqara Home app when paired with an Aqara Zigbee 3.0 hub. The lock also includes an auto-lock feature, configurable temporary access codes, and "Do Not Disturb" mode to mute sounds at specified times.


Designed for durability, the U50 is IPX5-rated for weather resistance, and it features a rekeyable lock cylinder for enhanced security and flexibility.


Key Features

  • Smart Automations with Aqara and Third-Party Devices: The Aqara U50 Smart Lock makes managing your home effortless by integrating seamlessly* with other smart home systems through the Zigbee protocol. It works beautifully with platforms like Google Home and Amazon Alexa, letting you create customised automations that simplify daily routines and enhance security. Whether it’s locking the door at bedtime or turning on the lights when you arrive, you’re in complete control.
  • Apple Home & Home Key Support: Unlocking your door has never been easier. With the Apple Home app or your Apple Watch, you can open the door instantly—no keys, no hassle, no unnecessary steps. Just a simple tap, and you’re in. Smart, fast, and effortless.
  • Door Status Sensing and Auto-Lock: Stay secure without lifting a finger. The U50 uses advanced gyroscope technology to detect door status and automatically lock once it’s closed. You can even set an alert sound in the app to notify you if the door hasn’t shut properly. Plus, with quiet mode for night-time and an always-open setting for busy spaces, the U50 adapts perfectly to both home and office environments.
  • Multiple and Emergency Unlocking Methods: Enjoy flexibility and peace of mind with several secure ways to unlock your door: keypad, physical key, NFC card, or USB-C for emergencies. The U50 integrates seamlessly with the Aqara Home app and leading smart home platforms, giving you total control and reassurance wherever you are.
  • Up to 6 Months of Battery Life: Designed with energy efficiency in mind, the U50 delivers up to 6 months* of usage on a single charge. Even if the battery runs low, USB-C emergency charging ensures you’re never locked out. Plus, the app sends timely battery replacement alerts—keeping your home secure without the stress of surprise power loss.
  • Built for Security and Durability: The U50 doesn’t just look smart—it’s built tough. Meeting BHMA Level 3 safety standards, it features a strong zinc alloy construction and an IPX5 water-resistance rating. Whether it’s rain, shine, or freezing conditions down to -18°C (-0.4°F), the U50 performs reliably year-round, making it perfect for any environment.

Note:
* Aqara Zigbee 3.0 hub is required.
** NFC card not included.
*** Based on the assumption of unlocking the door 8 times per day. Actual battery life may vary depending on usage.
