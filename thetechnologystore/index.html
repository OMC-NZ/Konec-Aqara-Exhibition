About Aqara

Aqara (pronounced Ah-KAR-uh) derives its name from the Latin words acutulus (smart) and ara (home), embodying its mission to make smart home technology accessible, elegant, and effortless. Since its founding in 2009, Aqara has become a global leader in innovation, empowering over 13 million users across 80+ countries. With a complete ecosystem of solutions—including sensors, switches, lights, controllers, cameras, hubs, and smart locks—Aqara delivers seamless automation, intelligent spatial awareness, and effortless integration with platforms like Matter, Thread, HomeKit, Google Home, and Alexa.

A simple Vision - to create living spaces that adapt to you.

Imagine your home anticipating your arrival—adjusting lighting, temperature, airflow, and even your favourite playlist before you step through the door. By combining AI, IoT, and big data, Aqara transforms buildings into intelligent environments that enhance comfort, security, and well-being while driving energy efficiency and financial savings.


With Aqara, the future of smarter, more sustainable living is here.
