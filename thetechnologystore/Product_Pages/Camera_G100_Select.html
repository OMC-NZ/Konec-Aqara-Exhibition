Product Info

Description

The Camera G100 Select is built for all-weather use, indoors or outdoors. With a sharp 2K resolution and a wide f/1.8 aperture, it delivers crisp, detailed images in any condition. Its spotlight and invisible infrared night vision modes ensure clear footage day or night.


For storage, you can choose what suits you best: 24/7 local recording with a microSD card (sold separately), NAS backup, or optional cloud storage – all designed with privacy and data protection in mind.


Packed with smart features such as AI-powered motion and human detection, plus custom ringtones for automations, it adds an extra layer of security to your home. The G100 Select works seamlessly with all major smart home ecosystems, including Apple Home, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, SmartThings, and Home Assistant (via RTSP), making it a powerful and flexible smart camera.


Key Features

  • 2K Clarity During Day and Night: The Camera G100 Select delivers 2K resolution with a wide f/1.8 aperture, capturing every detail in stunning clarity. Dual night modes – spotlight and invisible infrared – ensure vibrant, clear footage day or night, so nothing goes unnoticed, even in the darkest conditions.
  • HomeKit and 5-in-1 Ecosystem Support: Stream seamlessly via Wi-Fi to Apple Home (including HomeKit Secure Video¹), Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and SmartThings. It also integrates with Home Assistant via RTSP for advanced automation. With built-in 2-way audio, you can interact with visitors or communicate with family in real time, adding convenience and security.
  • Indoor or Outdoor with IP65 Protection: Designed for all-weather use, this IP65 waterproof camera works flawlessly both indoors and outdoors. Rain, wind, or sun won’t affect its performance, ensuring reliable protection wherever it’s placed.
  • 24/7 Local and E2E-Encrypted Storage²: The camera supports 24/7 local recording to microSD cards (up to 512GB, sold separately) and backup to local NAS servers³. Time-lapse capture⁴ compresses hours of footage into short, shareable videos, perfect for reviewing your day at a glance. With the optional HomeGuardian cloud service, recordings are end-to-end encrypted, and important events triggered by sensors are automatically highlighted, keeping your data secure and easy to access.
  • Local and Cloud AI Detection: Receive alerts only when needed with local AI detection for motion and human detection. For enhanced accuracy, Cloud AI detection⁵ recognises faces, vehicles, pets, and packages (requires a HomeGuardian subscription), offering advanced security features on par with high-end cameras.
  • 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi 6 with WPA3 Support: Equipped with 2.4GHz Wi-Fi, the camera ensures wide coverage, reliable performance, and strong anti-interference capability. WPA3 support provides enhanced network security for safe, worry-free operation.

Note:
* HomeKit Secure Video requires an iCloud plan and an Apple Home hub. When connected to both Apple Home and Aqara Home, resolution in Aqara Home is limited to 1080p; it returns to 1296p if HomeKit is disconnected.
** The cloud storage requires HomeGuardian plan, which has a free trial included and activated automatically.
*** NAS support requires inserting a microSD card (not included).
**** 24/7 continuous recording and time-lapse photography require a microSD card (not included) and are only available when the camera is powered via wired connection.
***** Cloud AI detection requires a HomeGuardian plan, which has a free trial included and is activated automatically.
