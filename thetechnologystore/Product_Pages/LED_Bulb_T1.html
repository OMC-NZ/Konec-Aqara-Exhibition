Product Info

Description

The Aqara Smart Light Bulb T1 is an energy-efficient lighting solution that offers adjustable white temperature, ranging from warm to cool, allowing users to customize their lighting for different moods and activities.


Compatible with Apple HomeKit, Google Assistant, and Amazon Alexa, it supports voice control and automation. The bulb fits standard E27 light sockets and can be easily controlled through the Aqara Home app or other compatible apps. It also features scheduling, timers, and group control for seamless smart home integration. With its long-lasting LED technology, the Aqara Smart Light Bulb T1 is a practical and customizable option for enhancing any smart home.


Key Features

  • Power Off Memory: Configure the bulb to automatically turn on after a power outage, preventing sudden lights at night.
  • More Connection Options:Connect via 5 GHz Wi-Fi, WPA3, or Ethernet with Aqara hubs like G3 Camera Hub and M2 Hub.
  • Ra90+ Colour Reproduction:Enhanced colour reproduction for cleaner whites, improving your environment and comfort.
  • Adjustable Transition Effects:Choose from lightning-fast to smooth transitions with precise customization in Aqara Home.
  • HomeKit Adaptive Lighting:Automatically adjusts colour temperature based on time of day for natural lighting.
  • Wide Ecosystem Support:Compatible with HomeKit, Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant, IFTTT, and more.
  • Bright 806 lm at 9W:806 lumens of bright white light, evenly diffused, while consuming just 9 watts.
  • Zigbee 3.0 & Matter Support:Fast, stable, and energy-efficient Zigbee 3.0 with upcoming Matter support.
