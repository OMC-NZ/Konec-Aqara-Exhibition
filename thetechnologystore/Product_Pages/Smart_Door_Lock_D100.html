Product Info

Description

The Aqara Smart Door Lock D100 offers seamless security and convenience with multiple unlocking options, including fingerprint, password, NFC card, mechanical key, and smartphone control via the Aqara Home app, Google Assistant, or Apple HomeKit.


Featuring a fully automatic lock body, it opens without needing to push a handle, and includes a premium glass-metal design, a 1-year rechargeable Li-Ion battery with USB-C charging, and support for Zigbee 3.0 and Bluetooth for smart home automation. With a loud 103 dB speaker, doorbell function, child lock switch, and a fast, ergonomic fingerprint reader with a sapphire coating, the D100 ensures both security and ease of use.


It fits doors of various thicknesses and offers backup options like a mechanical key and emergency charging, making it a reliable, advanced solution for any smart home.


Key Features

  • Fully-Automatic Lock: Opens automatically with no need to push the handle.
  • Google Assistant & HomeKit Support: Unlock with Google Assistant or Apple Home Key.
  • 1-Year Li-Ion Battery: Long-lasting with easy USB-C charging.
  • 9 Unlock Methods: Includes passwords, fingerprint, NFC, mechanical key, Aqara Home app, HomeKit, and Google Assistant.
  • Louder Speaker & Doorbell: 103 dB sound output and doorbell function.
  • Premium Metal Design: Solid, glass-metal unibody with no moving parts.
  • Wider Door Thickness Support:Fits doors 40-120 mm thick.
  • Mechanical Key & Emergency Charging: Backup options with mechanical key and Type-C port.
  • Zigbee 3.0 & Bluetooth: Reliable Zigbee for automation, Bluetooth for direct phone connection.
  • Keypad & Away Mode: Invisible keypad with Away Mode button for automations.
  • Remote Unlock via Aqara App: Unlock remotely and monitor deliveries.
  • Remotely Configurable Settings: Adjust passwords and settings remotely via Zigbee.
  • NFC Cards & Protection Covers: Includes two secure NFC cards and protective covers.
  • Ergonomic Fingerprint Reader: Fast and natural touch with sapphire coating.
  • Child Lock Switch:Prevents accidental unlocking by children
